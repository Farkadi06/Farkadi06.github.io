About us

Hassania GeoIT

Hassania GeoIT is a club within the Hassania School of Public Works which is responsible for promoting geographic information sciences.

  • Bring together all the students of EHTP who are concerned with the sciences of geographic information
  • Build a network linking engineering students with professionals in the field of GIS.
  • Represent the sector at the national level.
About the event

LET'S TALK GIS INTERNATINALLY!

Description

The event is in its 1st edition which will take place on February 06 at 18 GMT+1 in virtual form, and will be broadcast live on the club's Facebook page. "Let's talk GIS internationally" is considered one of the most important non-profit programs of the Hassania GEO-IT club to support geographic culture and to promote geographic information science, "The Science of Where", which helps to see, integrate, relive and understand all types of data in new ways through a common visual language.
